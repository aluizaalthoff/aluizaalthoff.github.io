Apple Photos redesign

UX Design
Toronto 2021
  • User Interface Design
  • User Experience Design
  • Brand identity
  • 3D mockups

With increasing storage capacity in mobile devices, our phones are now not only our personal cameras, but also the hub for the storage of photos and videos. Managing those files has become increasingly difficult. I decided to look at how Apple could improve their system, optimizing organization and search engines. Through interviews, research and reflecting about the issues users were going through, I understood that flexibility and customization were the key to offer a strong, user centered application.

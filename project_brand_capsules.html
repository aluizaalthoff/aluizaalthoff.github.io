Branding Capsules are compact design packages — logos and color systems tailored for startups and small businesses who need professional branding that fits their budget.

Mozzafiato

Mozzafiato is a coffee brand built on the idea of awakening the senses. The identity reflects sophistication and simplicity. It captures the luxury of pausing from daily demands, like a quiet morning in the mountains. A moment of reflection, stillness, and aroma.

A minimal coffee-mug form paired with handwritten script for intimacy, balanced by a modern sans serif that reinforces simplicity and the experience.

Rustic textures, serene landscapes, and cozy interiors that merge natural greens with coffee warmth, evoking duality and balance.

Earthy browns and crema tones contrasted with cool greens. Each color anchors serenity and richness, guiding the brand’s design language.

Handwritten script adds personality, while a clean sans serif provides structure.

From packaging to digital layouts, every touchpoint communicates an elevated pause: coffee as a moment of stillness and luxury.

Wood Floors Pro

Wood Floors Pro is a flooring company specializing in installation, refinishing, and custom solutions for hardwood, vinyl, laminate, trim, and plywood. The brand identity was designed to communicate craftsmanship, trust, and durability. By combining geometric precision with organic textures, the visual language conveys both technical expertise and the natural richness of wood.

The “WF” monogram is built with clean, structured lines that suggest both precision and professionalism. Its design also echoes the linear patterns of wood planks, reinforcing the company’s core expertise.

A collection of textures and references inspired by geometry, natural materials, and timeless craftsmanship. The imagery highlights strength, structure, and the enduring value of quality flooring.

Neutral stone, muted greens, warm wood tones, and deep browns form a palette rooted in nature and durability. These shades reinforce the brand’s connection to authenticity and resilience.

A sophisticated serif communicates elegance and heritage, while Gotham provides a modern, utilitarian complement. Together, they balance authority with clarity across all touchpoints.

From business cards to service communications, the identity system delivers consistency and professionalism. It reflects the company’s promise: high-quality flooring solutions built to last.

Sea swimwear

Sea Swimwear was created to embody lightness, simplicity, and the tropical spirit of summer. The brand reflects femininity and freshness, blending comfort with sophistication for special moments, from a beach luau to a boat party. Inspired by soft tones and candy colors the client loved, the identity conveys youth, quality, and a chic yet effortless style.

A flowing script that mirrors the movement of waves and the freedom of the sea.

Tropical landscapes, sunsets, and coastal living expressed through images that feel light and feminine. A youthful atmosphere that captures freedom and freshness.

Soft pastel shades and summer-inspired hues. The palette reflects delicacy, tropical energy, and the vibrancy of sunny days.

A modern handwritten typeface adds spontaneity and freshness, while Cinzel introduces elegance and structure, adding refinement to the brand’s youthful spirit.

From hang tags to packaging, the identity communicates lightness and style. Every detail reinforces the promise of quality and the feeling of experiencing summer with freedom and sophistication.

Hayanne Goston

Hayanne Goston is a wedding and event designer whose brand identity reflects elegance, warmth, and the artistry of celebration. Rooted in intimacy and timeless beauty, her visual language conveys sensitivity, merging natural inspiration, delicate typography, and a refined palette. This capsule captures a brand built to honor life’s most meaningful moments with grace and detail.

A hand-drawn floral illustration paired with a flowing script signature. The logo feels organic and personal, embodying Hayanne’s handcrafted approach to event design.

A visual world steeped in intimacy and nostalgia: vintage objects, soft florals, golden light, and textures that evoke timeless romance.

Neutral creams, vintage pinks, and muted greens form a palette that is both delicate and refined, grounding the brand in nature and sophistication.

A delicate script typeface reinforces the personal and artisanal spirit of the brand, while a sans serif supports clarity and practical usage across applications.

From stationery and business cards to event visuals, the identity translates into refined, elegant touchpoints that celebrate love and memory.

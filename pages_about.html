Ana Althoff

I've been working in design for over 13 years, and I still love every minute of it. My journey began in fashion, where I earned a BA and spent 8 years in Brazil's fashion industry learning how to manage complex production systems. That experience taught me to think systematically and work under pressure, skills that serve me well in brand design today.

After transitioning to visual design and studying Interactive Media Design, I found my passion for creating cohesive brand systems and design frameworks. At TLN Media Group, I've spent the past 3 years developing brand architectures for multicultural campaigns and leading creative direction for cultural events like the Salsa in Toronto festival.

I'm drawn to the intersection of brand strategy and experience design, where visual identity meets user interaction and where thoughtful systems enable teams to do their best work. I'm proactive, curious, and always exploring new ways to work smarter, including leveraging AI to improve design workflows. If that's what you're looking for, we might just be a perfect match. :)

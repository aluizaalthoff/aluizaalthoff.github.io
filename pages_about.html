About Me

I have been working with design for the past 8 years and I still love every minute of it.

At first I wanted to work in the fashion industry, I got a BA in Fashion, but one thing led to another and I started working as a surface designer. My eager and passion for learning drove me to move abroad to study Interactive media design. I’ve become obsessed with learning new softwares and producing visual content! I also love history and it’s easy to see in my work a touch of vintage styles. Bragging about myself, but being totally honest: I’m highly proactive, an eager learner and I give 100% of myself at each project in my life. If you are looking for a person like this, congratulations, you have found it! :)

Mozzafiato

Branding
Toronto 2020
  • Branding
  • 3D mockups

In collaboration with Mansy Ralhan, we developed the concept, identity, and brand book for a new coffee company, Mozzafiato, as part of a coursework project. Our goal was to create a sophisticated atmosphere and embrace the experience of “being in the moment.”

Concept

Sophistication and simplicity. It might seem dualistic. It’s quiet, it’s simple. It’s the new luxury of stepping away from demanding screens. It’s almost like meditation. What I picture is a morning in a cottage in the mountains, looking out the window at the gently curving landscape. The cold green tones against the warm, earthy colors of the coffee. There is indeed duality: hot and cold, elegance and simplicity. A moment of reflection, stillness, a true pause. A deep inhale—only the smell of coffee fills the room.

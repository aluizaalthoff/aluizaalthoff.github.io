Isabela Capeto + Stella Artois

Branding
Rio de Janeiro 2020
  • Branding
  • Illustrations

I led the visual identity development for the Isabela Capeto x Stella Artois collaboration—an exclusive capsule costume collection for the 2020 Carnival, inspired by Vintage Circus. I created a logo that merged both brands’ typographies into a single festive mark, and reimagined Isabela Capeto’s iconic doll with a Stella Artois star headpiece, bridging fashion and brand storytelling. I also produced a concept presentation book—illustrating the collection pieces and curating typography—for internal approval meetings with Stella Artois. The project was rounded out by designing branded tags and labels to ensure a consistent identity across all garments.
